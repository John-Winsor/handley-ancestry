Information from the above Birth Certificate (otherwise difficult to read):

Arthur Curle was born 19 November 1870 in Maryland Road, Stratford then in Essex.  His mother is Sarah Ann Curle then aged 17 (who is my great-grandmother).

The identity of Arthur's biological father is not indicated.  In Victorian times an unwanted pregnancy was likely to be regarded by the family as an economic disaster.  That was the basis, I suspect, of the Victorian mores that applied to such circumstances although the Victorians themselves may not have perceived it that way.

Sarah Ann Curle is referenced in the 1861 Census where she is listed simply as Sarah Curle aged 8.  Her father was Thomas Curle born about 1815 and her mother was Harriet Curle born about 1818.  Thomas Curle's occupation is given as "House Painter".  They lived in West Ham on the border with Stratford.  These people are my great-great-grandparents.
