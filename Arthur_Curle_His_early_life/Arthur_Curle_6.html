Notes:
1.  According to Ancestry.co.uk Walter John Handley (senior) died in early 1901 in Billericay, Essex aged 46 years.
     Hence Sarah Ann Handley is shown here as "Widow".
2.  Arthur and Walter John (junior) have married and left home.
3.  Eva Pattie Handley, absent in the 1891 Census, re-appears herewith.
4.  Kelly Blanche Handley, listed in the 1891 Census, is shown here as Kitty Blanche Handley.
5.  John Handley aged four is uncle to Arthur Thomas Handley then aged six (son of Arthur Handley).
6.  It is evident that Sarah Ann was mother to a total of nine children of which Arthur was the first and John was the last.
7.  The family has moved from West Ham.  The Census shows their address to be:
     Beaconsfield Villas, 175 High Road, Chadwell Heath, Dagenham, Essex.  This is in the registration district of Romford.

Death of Sarah Ann Handley née Curle:
Sarah Ann died at an address in Whalebone Lane, Chadwell Heath, Dagenham in June 1906 at the age of 52 years.
Eva Pattie Handley was present.  Perhaps this address was her home.
